## Research Area

This research focuses on the development of **interpretable and clinically applicable machine learning models** to predict **recurrence and survival outcomes** in breast cancer patients across multiple time horizons (2, 5, and 10 years).

The models are designed according to **TRIPOD** reporting guidelines and integrate clinical and hematological data to enhance prognostic accuracy. In this final module, the project completes the **multi-horizon framework** analysis, finalizes the manuscript for submission to *npj Breast Cancer (Nature)*, and prepares for international publication and academic career advancement.
